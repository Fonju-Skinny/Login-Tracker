How to us

You'll follow the below intructions step by step to creating a successful login credentials.

  1. Account Name:
    Type in the name of the application you Signed Up for, in the Account Name field so as to prevent similarity issues.
  2. Account URL:
    Copy the url of the application or website you Signed Up for, and paste it in the Account URL field. This will help you to navigate to the application quickly from here. It's Optional.
  3. Account Email:
    Copy the Email of the application you Signed Up for, and paste it in the Account Email field.
  4. Account Password:
    Copy the Password of the application or website you Signed Up for, and paste it in the Account Password field.
  5. Pick A Category:
    The Bussiness category is for the business accounts you create on the internet e.g LinkedIn, Glassdoor, Instagram, Google etc
    The Personal category is for the personal accounts you create on the internet.
  6. Add Account Button:
    Click the add acount button to generate your account credetials this button will take you to your logins list .
